The typical foods that originate from China have unique and savory flavors. All of the foods in China each have their unforgettable tastes and aspects that make them stand out from the rest of the dishes. Their taste has rich flavor that makes you reminice back to your childhood.

Xiao long bao (小笼包)is little soup
dumplings that are famous in China
and overseas. They are eaten at family gatherings, a celebration of some sorts, or even eating them just because they're so good.

Hot pot is world known. It has good flavor that you can choose and you put food items into the pot to cook in that flavor.

Char siu, also known as barbecued pork is a very famous dish known for its flavor.

Peking duck is duck with great flavors that can be eaten alone or paired with bread and cucumbers.

Tomato and egg is a dish
known for the savory
flavors the egg and tomato provide. They complement each other.

Tang yuan is glutinous rice balls with sweet or savory fillings. They are mainly eaten on Winter Solstice.

Moon cakes are eaten to celebrate Mid autumn festival.

No mi fan, or sticky rice, is glutinous rice with savory flavors that can be paired with Do fu hua. Sticky rice can be eaten on any occasion.

Do fu hua is tofu with soy milk to bring out the sweet flavor. It is commonly know to be paired with no mi fan because their flavors match each other.
