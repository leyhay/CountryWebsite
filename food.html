The typical foods that originate from China are Peking Duck, Char Siu, Xiao Long Bao and hot pot. Char Siu is barbecued pork and Xiao Long Bao is small steamed buns/dumplings.
Peking duck is one of the famous delicacies located in China because of its unique taste and savory flavors. All of the foods in China each have their unforgettable flavors and aspects that make them stand out from the rest of the dishes.
