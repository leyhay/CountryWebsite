The national flag of the People's Republic of China, also known as the Five Star Red Flag, has a big star in the corner and four stars surrounding it in an arc. The flag has five stars in the upper left corner. The big star represents the unity of Chinese people under the Chinese Communist Party(CCP) and the four other stars represents the four social classes.

China is located in East Asia. My three aspects about China are the food, traditions and the different celebrities that are located in China. In China, there are many delicacies and traditions that make China unique. There are the typical dishes and some dishes that are located or found more commonly in different provinces. Most of the traditions in China are the same in each province but they put their own twist on it. A province is a major administrative division within a country, similar to a state or county. There are different provinces that do things differently from one another. Each province is known for a certain celebrity.
