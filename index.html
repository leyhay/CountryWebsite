The national flag of the People's Republic of China, also known as the Five Star Red Flag, has a big star in the corner and four stars surrounding it in an arc.
The flag has five stars in the upper left corner. The big star represents the unity of Chinese people under the Chinese Communist Party(CCP) and the four other stars represents the four social classes.
