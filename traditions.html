China's Traditions

Home Food Traditions Celeberties History Gallery

The traditions of China are Chinese New Year Celebrations, The Mid-Autumn Festival, Dragon Boat Festival and Lantern Festival. China has 23 provinces, though the People's Republic of China (PRC) currently administers 22 of them. Each province has their own cultural identity. They are each celebrated in unique ways that make them more appreciated. There are also unspoken traditions of China like handing out red envelopes of money on birthdays and new years. It’s also a tradition to be kind and respectful to elders and never point your chopsticks up in the air.
